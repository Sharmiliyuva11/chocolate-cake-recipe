Chocolate Cake

🍽️ Watch the Recipe

⏱️ Prep Time: 45 mins | 🍽️ Serves: 8

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased baking tin.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
